# WordList

WordList is an iOS app designed to help users expand their vocabulary in a practical way. Instead of just saving interesting words and forgetting them later, the app keeps a personal word list and surfaces helpful word suggestions during writing.

The goal is to make vocabulary growth feel natural, useful, and tied to real writing habits.

## Features

- Save and organize words you want to learn or use more often
- Build a personal vocabulary list over time
- Get word suggestions while writing
- Reinforce stronger word choice through repeated exposure
- Simple, focused iOS experience built for everyday use

## Why I Built This

I wanted to create an app that helps people improve their vocabulary in a way that feels relevant and actionable. Many vocabulary tools focus on memorization, but this app focuses on application by helping users actually use the words they save in their own writing.

## How It Works

Users can add words to their personal list and refer back to them later. During writing, the app can suggest words from that list to encourage stronger, more varied language. This creates a feedback loop between learning new words and actively using them.
